## Project Description

Online question bank, paper generation, and online exams. Can automatically generate papers based on knowledge points, teaching materials, difficulty levels, etc., students complete answer sheets online within time limits.

## Key Features

- Student management
- Teaching material management
- Teacher management
- Course management (course categories, course levels, online courses)
- Live streaming management
- Live streaming history
- Knowledge points, subjects, assessment type management
- News management
- Question bank management (single choice, multiple choice, true/false)
- Batch question bank import
- Paper generation strategy and paper generation management
- Statistical reports and charts
- Permission system (roles and users)
- Operation logs
