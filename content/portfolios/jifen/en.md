## Project Description

Parent-child growth points, Android+iOS app, parents or teachers use point systems customized by experts for each age group to score children's daily behaviors and give rewards, achieving the goal of cultivating good behavioral habits in children.

## Key Features

- Point systems customized by education experts for each age group
- Points can be customized
- Scoring can be temporarily saved (confirmed by parents)
- Point passbook function (deposit and withdrawal)
- Can negotiate point rewards with children
- Points are divided into five dimensions: health, art, language, social, and science
- Beautiful interface, cultivating good behavioral habits in a relaxed and pleasant atmosphere with children
