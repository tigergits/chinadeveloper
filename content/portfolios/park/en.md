## Project Description

Manages park circular transformation indicators, including application, review, warning, query of park circular transformation indicators, and generating annual reports, etc.

## Key Features

- Parks log in with assigned accounts and fill in various park circular transformation indicators by year
- System automatically reviews, calculates, and warns on filled indicators
- Calculate indicator changes based on a certain year and warn
- Generate park circular transformation indicator annual reports
