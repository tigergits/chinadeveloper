## Project Description

Railway dispatcher simulation assessment system, starting from a train's operating plan, through the entire process of arrival, opening routes, and departure, assessing every step the dispatcher needs to take, every person to contact, every registration to make, all steps are recorded and scored, automatically forming an assessment form, with full voice operation.

## Key Features

- Customize vehicle operating plans
- Customize station maps
- Integrated iFlytek voice engine, each role can customize voice
- Fully simulates the dispatcher's working environment, including telephones, line occupancy board, operating plans, checklists, dispatch commands, assessment forms, etc.
- Full voice operation
- Can simulate various subject assessments, or customize subject assessment content
- Every step of the dispatcher during the assessment will be recorded and form a check score sheet
- Every step has scoring, automatically records the dispatcher's incorrect operations
