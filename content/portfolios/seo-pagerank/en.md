## Project Description

**SEO PageRank** analyzes any webpage in seconds and gives you a clear score out of 100.

**Chrome Store:** [SEO PageRank Extension](https://chromewebstore.google.com/detail/seo-pagerank/lndiecnlfaibiffoeijpjnblnmdlcpog)


### What We Check (18+ SEO Factors)

**Metadata** — Title tag, meta description, canonical URL
**Content Structure** — H1 uniqueness, heading hierarchy, image alt text
**Links** — Broken links, anchor text quality
**Technical SEO** — HTTPS, viewport, language, charset
**Social & Schema** — Open Graph, Twitter Cards, JSON-LD structured data

### Key Features

- **A-E Grade System** — Instantly see how your page ranks
- **Actionable Suggestions** — Know exactly what to fix and why
- **One-Click PDF Export** — Share reports with clients or teammates
- **Clean Interface** — No clutter, just insights

Perfect for SEO professionals, content creators, and web developers who want quick, reliable SEO checks without leaving the browser.
