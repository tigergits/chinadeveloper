## Project Description

This training management system includes a web-based training management system and a mini-program, suitable for large and medium-sized group enterprises and training schools for large-scale online and offline training or certification training. From training needs to the formulation and approval of training plans, to the full-process management of the entire training process, to forming one file per person and one file per session after class completion, automatically generating graduation certificates or qualification certificates.

The mini-program supports the needs of training and education functional departments, training and education managers of various subsidiaries, as well as group leaders, class teachers, teachers, and students. Different users logging in will enter different subsystems. Students can learn courses online, earn points, practice and take exams online, view their certificates, request leave, view their learning files, etc. Class teachers can view sign-ins uploaded from attendance machines in real-time, approve leave requests, logs, upload image materials, etc.

The system supports direct scanning of second-generation ID cards to automatically enter student information.

The system supports integration with attendance machines, can distribute student facial recognition information to attendance machines, supports fingerprint, face and ID card sign-in and attendance, attendance information is uploaded to the system and class teacher mini-program in real-time, and the system determines student lateness, early departure, and absenteeism based on custom sign-in templates.

## Key Features

- Organizational structure: Unit management, rules and regulations, teaching management personnel, full-time and part-time teachers, facility management, equipment management, attendance equipment management
- Teaching management: Training needs, training plans, class approval, training funds, training evaluation, annual comprehensive assessment, postgraduate activities
- Class management: Class management, teacher arrangement, student management, training notifications, sign-in rules, attendance management (sign-in sheets, original records, leave approval), class schedule management, lesson plan management, class teacher logs, exam management, student forum records, training result evaluation reports, image materials
- Personnel management: Personal information, personnel transfers (transfer applications, personnel receipt, transfer ledger), scanning entry
- File management: One file per session, safety qualification certificate management, employee external training records
- Statistical analysis: Personnel information statistics, plan summary, certification statistics, class information statistics
- System settings: Role management, backend management personnel, unit dictionary, personal information dictionary, sign-in templates, system logs, system maintenance
- ID card scanning program: Scan ID cards to directly enter employee (student) information
- Attendance machine: Integrated attendance machine, supports automatic sign-in, automatic determination of lateness, early departure, and absenteeism, supports facial recognition distribution, supports ID card, fingerprint, and facial recognition attendance methods
